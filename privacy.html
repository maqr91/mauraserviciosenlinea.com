Privacy Policy

Maura International Shipping & Services collects customer information in person at our Miami office when a shipment is created.

This information may include name, phone number, and shipment details.

Phone numbers are used only to send shipment notifications such as:

  • Package received
  • Shipment sent
  • Ready for pickup
  • Delivery confirmation

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Customers may reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance.

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
